AI Experience

Incorporating AI Thoughtfully

I actively work with modern AI technologies and continuously evaluate emerging tools. My goal is practical adoption — AI that genuinely improves how your team works, not tech for tech's sake.

🧠

AI Agents & Workflow Design

I design AI-powered workflows across Jira, JSM, and Jira Product Discovery — using agents and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) for intelligent request routing. The AI handles the repetitive work; your team stays in control of decisions.

🤝

Human-in-the-Loop Design

Good AI doesn't remove humans from important decisions — it gets them to the right decision faster. I build workflows with approval gates so AI handles triage and drafts while your team reviews what matters.

🔗

MCP & Tool Integration

From Atlassian Rovo to custom AI agents, I connect your tools using Model Context Protocol (MCP) so your systems share context intelligently — reducing copy-paste work and lost tribal knowledge.

📊

RAG Knowledge Systems

Turn your Confluence spaces and documentation into a queryable knowledge base. Your team stops searching and starts asking — and gets real answers from your own content.
